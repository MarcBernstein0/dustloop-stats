Characters


Guilty Gear -Strive- is the latest entry in the Guilty Gear franchise, featuring new and redesigned mechanics. Several characters from Guilty Gear XX and Guilty Gear Xrd have returned alongside many new characters.
DLC Characters

Season 1: Goldlewis Dickinson, Jack-O', Happy Chaos, Baiken and Testament—are available for individual purchase or as a part of the Season 1 Pass.

Season 2: Bridget, Sin Kiske, Bedman?, and Asuka R♯—are available for individual purchase or as a part of the Season 2 Pass.

Season 3: Johnny, Elphelt Valentine, A.B.A, and Slayer—are available for individual purchase or as a part of the Season 3 Pass.

Season 4: Queen Dizzy, Venom, Unika, and Guest character Lucy from Cyberpunk: Edgerunners are upcoming characters who have yet to be released.
